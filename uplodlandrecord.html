Required Document Checklist

1. Sale Deed / Title Deed

The primary legal document proving ownership transfer.

2. Mother Deed (Chain of Title)

All preceding deeds to trace the property's ownership history.

3. Encumbrance Certificate (EC)

Certifies the property is free from legal or monetary dues.

4. Latest Property Tax Receipts

Receipts proving all property taxes have been paid to date.

5. Khata Certificate / Extract

Identifies the property owner for tax payment purposes.

6. Approved Layout Plan / Survey Sketch

Official map from survey dept. or planning authority.

7. Conversion Order (NA Order)

Required if land use was converted (e.g., Agricultural to Non-Agricultural).

8. Owner's Identity & Address Proof

Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID etc.
