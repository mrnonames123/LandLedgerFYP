Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our process

How long does the verification process take?

Our AI-powered verification typically completes within 24-48 hours. Complex cases may take up to 5 business days.

What documents do I need to upload?

You'll need property deeds, identity documents, and any relevant legal paperwork. Our system guides you through required documents.

Is my data secure during the process?

Yes, we use military-grade encryption and follow strict data protection protocols to ensure your information remains secure.

Can I modify my information after verification?

Yes, you can update your information through your dashboard. Some changes may require re-verification for security purposes.
